Research Interests & Current Project

Research Interests

My primary research interests include multilingual STEM education, curriculum studies, and plurilingualism, with a focus on creating equitable and culturally responsive learning environments for immigrant and international students [1-3].

Current Project: Agentic AI Multilingual Math Practice Tracker

This project integrates my research and technical skills to address critical needs for multilingual learners in mathematics [8-11].

  • Conducted foundational research to identify critical pain points for multilingual students in STEM, revealing a need for language support in math problem-solving.
  • Translated qualitative insights into design concepts, focusing on accessibility and comprehension for newcomer learners [8-11].
  • Designed and built an interactive prototype using Python, Django, and the Gemini API to validate research findings and test a core feature: enabling users to record math problems with translated explanations [8, 9].
